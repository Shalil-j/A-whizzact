Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is an umbrella term for the marketing of products or services using digital technologies, mainly on the Internet, but also including mobile phones, display advertising, and any other digital medium.

Internet marketing services involve the promotion of products, services, or brands through digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. These services use a variety of tactics, including search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, social media marketing, and content marketing, to drive traffic, generate leads, and increase conversions.
